# Professional Service Offer: AI Trust & Security Readiness for Internal Knowledge Assistants

## Service Summary
A structured service to assess and improve trust, security, and governance readiness for enterprise internal AI knowledge assistants built with Onyx.

## Ideal Clients
- Mid-size to enterprise organizations deploying internal RAG assistants
- Teams requiring security review before broad employee rollout
- Organizations with audit, legal, or governance obligations

## What Is Included
1. Readiness scoping and assumptions workshop
2. Architecture, trust-boundary, and data-flow analysis
3. Threat modeling and abuse-case development
4. Control design and retrofit recommendations
5. Evidence-driven test planning and execution support
6. Launch gate decision package and residual risk register
7. Governance and continuous review operating model

## What Is Not Claimed
- Guaranteed prevention of all model or system failures
- One-time assessment as a substitute for continuous governance
- Automatic compliance certification without client-side controls

## Engagement Model
- **Phase 1 (Discovery):** scope, inventory, assumptions
- **Phase 2 (Assessment):** threats, controls, verification design
- **Phase 3 (Validation):** testing, evidence, launch gate support
- **Phase 4 (Operationalization):** governance, runbook, review cadence

## Typical Outputs
- Control matrix and ownership map
- Abuse-case test report
- Evidence inventory with confidence labels
- Launch decision briefing and prioritized backlog

## Client Adoption Path
1. Pilot with narrow department scope
2. Validate controls and evidence quality
3. Expand to additional repositories and user groups
4. Re-run launch gate with updated risk profile
