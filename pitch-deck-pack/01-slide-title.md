## Slide purpose
Explain this stage of the readiness story with clear claim boundaries.

## Main message
AI systems need evidence before they are trusted to retrieve sensitive data or call tools.

## Visual idea

## Talk track
Focus on evidence boundaries, launch-gate logic, and what is still blocked without real client evidence.

## Key bullets
- Subtitle: Evidence-based readiness, runtime controls, and launch-gate decisions for AI systems that retrieve, reason, and act.
- Three pillars: RAG Readiness, Agent Readiness, Launch Gates
- Most AI demos prove answering; this project evaluates retrieve/act/launch permission.
