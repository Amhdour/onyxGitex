## Slide title
Next Step: Client Discovery and Evidence Intake

## Slide purpose
Explain this stage of the readiness story with clear claim boundaries.

## Main message
The next step is to apply the template to a real client system, collect real evidence, and make a defensible launch-gate decision.

## Visual idea
Use a simple consulting-style diagram with neutral palette and minimal iconography.

## Talk track
Focus on evidence boundaries, launch-gate logic, and what is still blocked without real client evidence.

2. AI system scope
3. Data and tool inventory
4. RAG and agent risk mapping
5. Evidence collection
6. Runtime tests
7. Observability review
8. Residual risk review
9. Launch-gate decision
- Call to action: start focused readiness assessment for one AI system/use case.
