# 14 Client Questions and Answers

1. Is this production-ready? No; it is a readiness methodology and evidence framework.
2. Can you secure our RAG assistant? I can assess and improve controls, then provide evidence-based recommendations.
3. Can you secure our agents? I can evaluate tool governance and policy boundaries with testable evidence.
4. Do you replace our cybersecurity team? No.
5. Do you replace compliance/legal review? No.
6. Can this work with our existing stack? Usually yes with adaptation.
7. Do we need Onyx specifically? No, methodology is portable.
8. Can this work with Dify, LangGraph, Open WebUI, Flowise? Yes as a readiness model, with client-specific integration work.
9. What evidence do you need from us? Runtime logs, policies, identities, tools, traces, and approvals.
10. What do we get at the end? Evidence pack and gate decision support.
11-14. Launch gate terms define decision outcomes from NO-GO to GO based on evidence completeness.
15. How long is first assessment? Typically weeks, depending scope.
16. Can you train our team? Yes.
17-18. Can this help before/after deployment? Yes.
19. Biggest risk? Unauthorized retrieval or tool action without traceable controls.
20. Next step? Discovery and scoped readiness sprint.
