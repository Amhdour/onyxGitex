# Client-Facing Walkthrough Pack

## 1. Purpose
This walkthrough pack explains the onyxGitex AI Trust & Security Readiness evidence chain. It is designed for client conversations, partner conversations, and portfolio demonstrations. It does not claim production readiness or client launch approval.

## 2. Who this is for
- Business owners, founders, CTOs, CIOs, and AI product leaders
- Cybersecurity consultants, risk managers, and compliance teams
- AI agencies, startup teams, and RAG/agent builders
- University and private-school decision makers

## 3. What this pack explains
- The problem onyxGitex addresses for RAG and autonomous agents
- What each project version proves and does not prove
- How evidence is collected and used for launch-gate decisions
- How to apply Version 4 with real client-specific evidence
