# 10 Client Meeting Talk Track

“Most AI demos answer the question: can the system respond? This project asks a harder question: should the system be allowed to respond, act, retrieve, call tools, and launch?”

## For CEO/founder
Focus on risk-adjusted speed and decision clarity.
## For CTO
Focus on architecture boundaries, evidence chain, and rollout controls.
## For security lead
Focus on fail-closed controls, abuse-case coverage, and traceability.
## For compliance/risk
Focus on audit evidence, residual risk, and approval records.
## For AI engineer
Focus on test harnesses, policy checks, and runtime scenarios.
## For university/private school leader
Focus on safe internal knowledge workflows and governance maturity.
## For AI agency partner
Focus on adding trust/evidence deliverables to existing AI offers.
