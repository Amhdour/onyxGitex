# 15 Objection Handling

- “We already tested the chatbot.” Response: Great start; readiness adds authorization, observability, and launch-gate evidence.
- “We already have cybersecurity.” Response: This complements existing security with AI-specific runtime and retrieval controls.
- “We do not need governance.” Response: Governance prevents unclear ownership during incidents and launch decisions.
- “The AI vendor says it is safe.” Response: Vendor assurances still need client-specific evidence and risk acceptance.
- “We are not ready for a full audit.” Response: Start with a focused readiness sprint.
- “We do not have a big budget.” Response: Begin with highest-risk paths first.
- “We need to launch fast.” Response: Fast launches benefit from explicit NO-GO triggers and minimum evidence.
- “We do not use Onyx.” Response: Methodology is platform-agnostic.
- “We use agents, not RAG.” Response: Agent governance is directly included.
- “We are worried about exposing data.” Response: Retrieval boundaries and audit trails are core priorities.
