# Final Delivery Checklist (Client-Facing)

Use this checklist at closeout to verify engagement completeness and launch-decision readiness.

## 1) Scope & Governance
- [ ] Final scope aligns with signed SOW/proposal
- [ ] Out-of-scope items documented and acknowledged
- [ ] Decision owners and sign-off path confirmed

## 2) Threats, Abuse Cases, and Controls
- [ ] Threat model reviewed with stakeholders
- [ ] Abuse-case catalog reviewed and prioritized
- [ ] Retrieval authorization controls documented
- [ ] Fail-closed rules documented and validated
- [ ] Layer Retrofit plan delivered
- [ ] Secure Starter Kit integration guidance delivered

## 3) Verification & Evidence
- [ ] Test plan executed or explicitly deferred with rationale
- [ ] Results recorded with command/test references
- [ ] Findings labeled Verified / Partially Confirmed / Unknown
- [ ] Evidence traceability links code/config/test/log artifacts
- [ ] Residual risk register completed

## 4) Launch Gate Readiness
- [ ] Launch criteria reviewed (Go / Conditional Go / No-Go)
- [ ] Open critical risks enumerated with owner and due date
- [ ] Decision memo prepared for governance body
- [ ] Launch Gate decision recorded with date and approver

## 5) Handoff & Continuity
- [ ] Operational runbook updates completed
- [ ] Control ownership and review cadence assigned
- [ ] Follow-up roadmap for unresolved items agreed
