# Retainer Service Model (Client-Facing)

## Purpose
Define an ongoing advisory model for AI Trust & Security Readiness after initial assessment and launch-gate support.

## Scope Boundary
Retainer services provide governance, validation support, and evidence upkeep. They do not replace client operational ownership or guarantee risk elimination.

## Retainer Tiers (Draft)

| Tier | Monthly Focus | Typical Activities |
|---|---|---|
| Essential | Governance stability | Monthly risk review, control ownership checks, evidence hygiene |
| Advanced | Control maturity | Abuse-case refresh, targeted control testing, policy logging review |
| Strategic | Change readiness | Architecture change impact review, launch-gate rehearsal, executive reporting |

## Core Retainer Services
1. **Control Health Reviews**
   - Validate whether key controls remain in effect
   - Track drift in retrieval authorization and identity boundaries

2. **Evidence Maintenance**
   - Keep evidence pack current with updated artifacts
   - Reclassify findings as Verified / Partially Confirmed / Unknown as systems evolve

3. **Change Risk Assessments**
   - Evaluate impact of new tools, models, connectors, and agent capabilities
   - Update Layer Retrofit and Secure Starter Kit guidance as needed

4. **Launch Gate Support for Major Changes**
   - Re-run targeted readiness checks for major releases
   - Facilitate conditional launch criteria and risk acceptance decisions

## Service Cadence
- Monthly working session
- Quarterly deep-dive assessment
- Ad hoc escalation advisory (bounded SLA per contract)

## Inputs Required from Client
- Roadmap and release changes
- Updated architecture/control artifacts
- Access to relevant logs and test outputs
- Named decision owners

## Retainer Outcomes
- Reduced readiness drift
- Faster and more defensible launch decisions
- Continuous audit trail for governance and compliance stakeholders

## Commercial Structure (Draft)
- Monthly fee bands linked to system complexity and review depth
- Optional pre-purchased surge capacity for major launch events
- Annual term with quarterly value checkpoints
