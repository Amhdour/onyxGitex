# Delivery Timeline Template (Client-Facing)

## Purpose
Template to communicate timeline, milestones, and readiness checkpoints for AI Trust & Security Readiness engagements.

## Scope Boundary
This template plans advisory and assurance work. It does not assume automatic deployment approval.

## Engagement Timeline (Template)

| Week | Phase | Primary Activities | Client Inputs | Outputs |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Client Intake | Stakeholder kickoff, objectives, constraints | Sponsor goals, key contacts | Intake summary |
| 2 | AI System Scope | System boundaries, use cases, trust boundaries | Architecture artifacts | Scope definition |
| 3 | Inventory & Assumptions | Asset/dependency inventory, assumptions | System owners, vendor list | Inventory + assumptions log |
| 4-5 | Threat Modeling & Abuse Cases | Threat paths, abuse patterns, misuse scenarios | Technical walkthroughs | Threat model + abuse catalog |
| 6-7 | Controls & Layer Retrofit | Control selection, Layer Retrofit, Secure Starter Kits mapping | Security standards, control owners | Control matrix + retrofit plan |
| 8 | Observability & Audit Design | Audit events, runtime tracing, decision logging design | Logging stack details | Observability blueprint |
| 9-10 | Testing & Verification | Control tests, abuse-case validation, evidence review | Test environment access | Verification summary |
| 11 | Evidence Pack | Traceability, residual risk documentation | Final evidence inputs | Evidence pack draft |
| 12 | Launch Gate Decision | Readiness scoring and decision facilitation | Decision stakeholders | Launch Gate memo |

## Milestone Checkpoints
- M1: Scope Baseline Approved
- M2: Threat & Control Baseline Approved
- M3: Verification Evidence Accepted
- M4: Launch Gate Decision Recorded

## Timeline Risks & Dependencies
- Delayed stakeholder access
- Incomplete logging or audit data
- Scope expansion without reprioritization

## Status Codes
- Green: on-track
- Amber: risk identified, mitigation in progress
- Red: timeline/quality impact likely

## Change Control
All timeline shifts require:
1. documented reason,
2. impact on deliverables,
3. revised decision date.
