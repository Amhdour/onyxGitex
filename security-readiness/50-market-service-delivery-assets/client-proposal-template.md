# Client Proposal Template (AI Trust & Security Readiness)

## 1. Executive Summary
[Client Name] seeks to evaluate and strengthen AI Trust & Security Readiness for internal RAG and autonomous agent capabilities. This proposal presents an evidence-first delivery model focused on launch decisions, risk reduction, and governance maturity.

## 2. Objectives
- Prevent sensitive data leakage and identity-boundary bypass.
- Reduce unsupported model outputs and unsafe tool behavior.
- Establish auditable evidence for launch-gate decisions.
- Define residual risk and governance operating cadence.

## 3. In-Scope Services
- Discovery, scope, and assumptions alignment
- Threat modeling and abuse-case development
- Controls selection including retrieval authorization and fail-closed patterns
- Layer Retrofit and Secure Starter Kit integration planning
- Verification strategy, evidence packaging, and launch-gate facilitation

## 4. Out-of-Scope
- Legal advisory or regulatory interpretation
- Full production implementation of all remediation controls
- Managed SOC operations or 24/7 monitoring

## 5. Delivery Approach
1. Baseline the system and trust boundaries.
2. Identify abuse paths and control gaps.
3. Prioritize controls and retrofit strategy.
4. Validate through testing and evidence review.
5. Facilitate Launch Gate decision.

## 6. Deliverables
- Threat model and abuse-case catalog
- Controls matrix and ownership mapping
- Observability and audit event requirements
- Verification summary and residual risk register
- Evidence pack and launch decision memo

## 7. Evidence & Decision Model
Findings are explicitly classified as Verified, Partially Confirmed, or Unknown. Launch decision options:
- **Go**: critical controls verified and residual risk accepted
- **Conditional Go**: bounded risks accepted with dated mitigation commitments
- **No-Go**: unresolved high-impact risks or missing critical evidence
