# Pricing Model Draft (Client-Facing)

> **Draft Notice:** This document is a planning draft for service estimation and is **not financial advice**.

## Purpose
Provide transparent draft pricing structures for AI Trust & Security Readiness services covering RAG and autonomous agents.

## Scope Boundary
- Pricing covers advisory, documentation, control design, and verification support.
- Pricing excludes implementation-heavy engineering rebuilds unless explicitly scoped.

## Draft Pricing Structures

### 1) Phase-Based Pricing (Recommended)
Best for clients executing sequential readiness phases.

| Package | Example Scope | Draft Fee Range (USD) |
|---|---|---:|
| Foundation | Intake, scope, inventory, assumptions | $18,000 - $35,000 |
| Risk & Controls | Threat modeling, abuse cases, controls, retrofit plan | $35,000 - $85,000 |
| Verification & Launch Gate | Testing framework, evidence pack, readiness decision | $30,000 - $70,000 |

### 2) Fixed-Sprint Pricing
Best for targeted outcomes in 2-4 week increments.

| Sprint Type | Duration | Draft Fee Range (USD) |
|---|---:|---:|
| Discovery Sprint | 2 weeks | $12,000 - $20,000 |
| Threat & Controls Sprint | 3 weeks | $22,000 - $38,000 |
| Evidence Sprint | 2 weeks | $15,000 - $28,000 |

### 3) Program Bundle Pricing
Best for end-to-end readiness across the full lifecycle.

- Draft bundle range: **$95,000 - $240,000**
- Typical duration: 8-16 weeks
- Includes launch-gate decision facilitation

## Price Drivers
- Number of AI systems/environments
- Identity model complexity and trust boundaries
- Third-party dependency count
- Depth of red teaming and verification expectations
- Required turnaround timeline

## Commercial Assumptions
- Client provides timely access to architecture, policies, and stakeholders.
- Rework from shifting scope may trigger change orders.
- High-severity remediation implementation is estimated separately.

## Value Anchors
- Reduced launch risk through evidence-backed controls
- Faster executive decisions through launch-gate clarity
- Audit-ready artifacts for security and governance stakeholders

## Draft Discounts (Optional)
- Multi-phase commitment: up to 8% draft discount
- Annual retainer + initial assessment: up to 10% draft discount

## Final Pricing Governance
Final commercial terms are confirmed only after discovery and written scope acceptance.
