# External Reviewer Checklist

## Purpose
Support independent external review of AI Trust & Security Readiness artifacts and evidence quality.

## Independence Requirements
- Reviewer must not be the control implementer.
- Reviewer must document assumptions and limitations.
- Any unverified claim must be marked `Unknown` or `Partially Confirmed`.

## Checklist

| ID | Review Criteria | Evidence Required | Reviewer | Status | Finding Severity | Remediation | Sign-off |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ER-01 | System scope and in-scope components are explicitly defined. | Architecture scope docs, component inventory, version/date stamps. | External Reviewer | Not Started | N/A | N/A | Pending |
| ER-02 | Data classification aligns to handling controls. | Data classification matrix, access policies, sampling evidence. | External Reviewer | Not Started | N/A | N/A | Pending |
| ER-03 | Threat model covers abuse cases and attack paths. | Threat model document, abuse-case list, mitigation mapping. | External Reviewer | Not Started | N/A | N/A | Pending |
