# AI Risk Disclaimer Template

> **Non-Legal Draft Notice (Required Lawyer Review):**
> This document is an operational draft template and is **not legal advice**. It must be reviewed, revised, and approved by qualified legal counsel before use in any contract, statement of work, order form, or client-facing commitment.

## Purpose
This template defines baseline AI risk disclosure language for internal knowledge-assistant deployments so parties have a shared understanding of limitations, assumptions, and residual risk.

## Template Language
"The AI-enabled knowledge assistant service is provided with security, governance, and quality controls designed to reduce risk; however, no AI system can guarantee complete accuracy, completeness, or uninterrupted availability in all circumstances.

Customer acknowledges that:
1. AI outputs may include errors, omissions, stale information, or contextually incorrect interpretations.
2. Outputs may require independent validation by qualified personnel before business, legal, regulatory, financial, or safety-critical use.
3. Risk controls are effective only when configured, operated, monitored, and reviewed according to agreed operational procedures.
4. Residual risk remains after control implementation, including risks from model behavior, data quality, third-party components, and novel attack methods.

Provider does not represent that the AI system will detect all policy violations, prevent all unauthorized access attempts, or provide deterministic outcomes for every query or workflow." 

## Operational Notes
- Use this disclaimer where AI-generated content may be used to inform internal decisions.
- Pair with documented control evidence and test results.
- Ensure references to controls are specific to implemented capabilities, not aspirational features.
