# Scope Limitation Clause

> **Non-Legal Draft Notice (Required Lawyer Review):**
> This is a non-legal operational draft and **not legal advice**. Final enforceable wording requires attorney review.

## Purpose
Define explicit operational boundaries of the readiness effort and the deployed assistant to avoid implied commitments outside tested scope.

## Template Language
"The AI Trust & Security Readiness activities and resulting deliverables are limited to the expressly defined system scope, environments, integrations, controls, and test conditions documented in the associated readiness artifacts.

Unless explicitly stated in writing, this work does not include:
1. Certification, legal compliance determination, or formal regulatory attestation.
2. Guarantees of security against all present or future threats.
3. Validation of systems, datasets, endpoints, or third-party services not identified within approved scope.
4. Continuous monitoring obligations beyond agreed operational periods.
5. Suitability determinations for high-risk legal, medical, safety-critical, or financial decision automation.

Any use of the AI assistant outside the documented scope, assumptions, configuration baseline, or control envelope is at Customer risk and requires separate assessment." 

## Operational Notes
- Keep scope references mapped to versioned readiness documents.
- Include environment boundaries (e.g., staging vs production).
- Include explicit exclusions for out-of-scope connectors and data domains.
