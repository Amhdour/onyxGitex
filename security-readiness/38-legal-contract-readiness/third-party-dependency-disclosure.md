# Third-Party Dependency Disclosure

> **Non-Legal Draft Notice (Required Lawyer Review):**
> This disclosure template is a non-legal draft and **not legal advice**. Obtain legal review before use in contracts or procurement materials.

## Purpose
Disclose material third-party dependencies that can affect security, availability, privacy, and contractual risk.

## Template Language
"The AI assistant service may rely on third-party software, infrastructure, APIs, model providers, and security tooling. These dependencies may materially affect system behavior, data handling, and risk posture.

Provider will make commercially reasonable efforts to:
1. Maintain an inventory of material third-party dependencies.
2. Communicate material dependency changes that may affect security or service behavior.
3. Apply risk-based review and patching practices for known vulnerabilities.
4. Track dependency-related incidents and relevant advisories.

Customer acknowledges that:
1. Certain dependency risks are outside Provider’s direct operational control.
2. Upstream outages, policy changes, model deprecations, or contractual changes may impact service capabilities.
3. Dependency-specific assurances may be limited by upstream transparency and contractual terms.

Dependency risk acceptance, procurement decisions, and legal approvals remain subject to Customer governance and legal review."
