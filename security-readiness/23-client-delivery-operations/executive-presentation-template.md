# Executive Presentation Template

## Slide 1: Engagement Objective
- What was assessed
- Why it matters to operations and risk
- Decision needed from leadership

## Slide 2: Scope and Method
- In-scope systems, users, and data
- Time period assessed
- Methods used (interviews, config review, testing, log validation)

## Slide 3: Current Risk Posture
- Top risks by impact and likelihood
- Severity distribution
- Notable trend since prior checkpoint

## Slide 4: Control Effectiveness Summary
- Controls Verified
- Partially Confirmed controls
- Unknown control areas requiring more evidence

## Slide 5: Top Findings and Business Impact
- 3–5 highest-priority findings
- Practical business consequence by sector context
- Current mitigation status

## Slide 6: Remediation Plan and Owners
- Actions, owners, dates
- Dependencies and decision bottlenecks
- Support needed from leadership

## Slide 7: Launch Gate Recommendation
- Recommendation: Go / Go with Risk Acceptance / No-Go
- Conditions required for a safe launch path
- Explicit residual risks and accountable approvers

## Slide 8: Decision Record
- Leadership decision captured
- Accepted risks and expiration/review date
- Follow-up review cadence

## Evidence Limitation Language (Client-Facing)
This presentation summarizes assessment evidence available as of the reporting date. It does not guarantee detection of all vulnerabilities or future incidents.

## Launch Gate Decision Language (Client-Facing)
A **Go** recommendation requires sufficient verified evidence for required controls. Where evidence is incomplete or significant findings remain, decision options should be **Go with Risk Acceptance** or **No-Go** with documented rationale.
