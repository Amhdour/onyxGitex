# Demo Script (Portfolio Presentation)

## Audience
Hiring managers, security leaders, and engineering peers evaluating AI trust/security execution depth.

## Narrative
"This is a fictional Atlas Advisory Group readiness engagement on top of Onyx. The goal was to determine whether an internal RAG assistant could launch safely without overclaiming confidence."

## Script Outline
1. State mission and constraints (no overclaiming, evidence-first).
2. Show lifecycle coverage from scope to governance.
3. Walk through control architecture and fail-closed stance.
4. Show testing strategy (abuse cases + red teaming + retrieval boundaries).
5. Show observability and audit-readiness artifacts.
6. Show launch-gate decision logic and residual risks.
7. Conclude with what is proven vs not proven.

## Key Talking Points by Competency
- Layer Retrofit: security controls integrated around existing product architecture.
- Secure Starter Kits: reusable templates/artifacts for future AI initiatives.
- Launch Gates: explicit decision criteria and hold/approve logic.
- Evals & Red Teaming: structured adversarial validation approach.
- Policy-as-Code: rules and logs over implicit behavior.
- Retrieval Security: boundary-aware retrieval authorization model.
- Agent/Tool/MCP Hardening: non-LLM surfaces treated as first-class risk vectors.
- Telemetry & Auditability: evidence trail for incident readiness.

## Mandatory Disclaimers
- Fictional client context: Atlas Advisory Group.
- No claim of real production deployment.
- Not Proven items are explicitly retained.
