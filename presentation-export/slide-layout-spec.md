# Slide Layout Specification

- Slide size: 16:9 widescreen.
- Typography: Aptos preferred; fallback Arial, Calibri, Inter.
- Color palette: black (#111111), dark gray (#333333), medium gray (#777777), light gray (#EAEAEA), white (#FFFFFF).
- Spacing: generous margins (5-7%), 8pt baseline spacing, avoid dense paragraphs.
- Footer style: single-line muted text at bottom; required boundary text on all slides.
- Title style: bold, high-contrast, left-aligned.
- Body text style: concise bullets, sentence case, no more than 5 bullets per slide.
- Diagram style: thin lines, simple shapes, no screenshots/logos/photos.

Per-slide layout recommendations:
1. Title + three-pillar strip.
2. Split comparison grid.
3. Horizontal process pipeline.
4. Vertical maturity ladder.
5. Boundary diagram with policy gate.
6. Evidence feedback loop.
7. Tool-authorization flowchart.
8. Service-map block architecture.
9. Evidence matrix table.
10. Engagement timeline path.
